The purpose ofthis engine is to prove that Anti-Line4 is a second-player win.
Anti-Line 4 is played with the rules known from Brainking and Zillions of games:
1. A player is not allowed to move on a column where his opponent has moved in his last turn
2. If the board is full and no player has 4 pieces in a row, the first player wins.
(https://brainking.com/en/GameRules?tp=14)
